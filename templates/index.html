An online registration system was developed where a student can register himself

Online Course Registration System is Web-based registration software that helps you to register courses online.

  • Making the registration process accessible anywhere to the student.
  • The registration form has been designed to be user friendly and easy to fill and hence leads to saving of time and money as compared to multiple forms filled manually by the students.
  • The student course registration portal accepts input in the form of student’s personal detail, student’s registration details, faculty details etc

Development of Student registration system has greatly simplified previously complex tasks. Manual work is reduced to a large extent. The task of maintaining huge data can now be accomplished with a few button clicks.
